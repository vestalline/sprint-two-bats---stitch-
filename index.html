The Fruit Bat: Greedy Little Creatures

menu_book About the Species

Megabats - Otherwise Known as Fruit Bats (Pteropus medius) Are a species of bats that contain the largest bats in the world - weighing up to more than three pounds and wingspans of over five and a half feet. There are many different types of Fruit Bats however, so size varies incredibly.

info Quick Facts

  • Wingspan 167 cm (65 in)
  • Weight 3.5 pounds
  • Lifespan Up to 30 years
  • Status Slight Concern

Global Distribution

Located within the tropics of the "Old World", as well as the indian ocean and Oceania.
